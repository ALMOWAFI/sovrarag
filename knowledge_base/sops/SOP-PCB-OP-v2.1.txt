STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE
SOP-PCB-OP-v2.1: Open Defect (OP) on Conductor Paths
Revision: 2.1 | Effective Date: 2025-09-15 | Owner: Quality Engineering

1. SCOPE
This SOP covers the identification, disposition, and escalation of Open (OP) defects detected on PCB conductor paths during AOI or electrical inspection.

2. DEFINITION
An Open (OP) defect is a break or discontinuity in a conductor trace, pad, or via connection that interrupts the intended electrical path. Open defects are critical for circuit functionality and reliability.

3. SEVERITY CLASSIFICATION
- Open defects on conductor paths are critical. They directly affect electrical continuity and circuit function.
- Open defects in functional conductor regions (power distribution, signal paths, safety-critical circuits) are classified as highest severity.
- Open defects in non-functional regions (test coupons, fiducial areas) are lower severity but must still be documented.

4. DISPOSITION RULES

4.1 Single Occurrence:
- Route to manual inspection to confirm the open is real (not a false detection).
- If confirmed, evaluate whether the affected conductor is in a functional zone.
- Functional zone opens: reject or route to engineering-controlled rework.
- Non-functional zone opens: log and monitor.

4.2 Repeated OP Defects on Same Line or Board Region:
- Repeated OP defects on the same line or board region require engineering escalation and process review.
- Automatic pass is not allowed for repeated OP defects in functional conductor regions.
- Investigate etching process parameters, chemical bath condition, and exposure settings.

4.3 Post-Repair Recurrence:
- If an OP defect recurs after manual repair (e.g., copper bridge repair), the repair is considered ineffective.
- Manual bridge repair may restore continuity but does not address the root cause of repeated conductor formation or etching instability.
- Escalate to engineering for process root cause analysis.

5. ESCALATION
- Single confirmed OP in functional zone: reject or engineering rework.
- Recurring OP (2+ cases in 90 days on same region): escalate_engineering.
- OP recurrence after repair: escalate_engineering — do not approve another manual repair without process review.
