TECHNICAL MANUAL
TECH-PCB-X100-ZONE-MAP: PCB-X100 Board Zone Mapping
Product: PCB-X100 | Applicable Revisions: Rev-B, Rev-C

1. PURPOSE
This document defines the board zone map for PCB-X100, separating the functional circuit area from the process monitoring test coupon area. This mapping is used for zone-based defect disposition.

2. ZONE DEFINITIONS

2.1 Functional Circuit Area:
- Encompasses all active conductor traces, component pads, vias, and plated through-holes that are part of the final product circuit.
- Includes: BCM-01 power distribution, lighting control signal paths, communication bus routing, component mounting areas.
- Defects in this zone directly affect product functionality and reliability.

2.2 Test Coupon Area:
- Located on breakaway tabs or designated corners of the production panel.
- The detected HB coordinates belong to the test coupon area, which is used for process monitoring and is not part of the functional circuit.
- Test coupons contain representative conductor patterns, vias, and plated holes that mirror production features.
- Test coupons are used for:
  - Process capability monitoring (drill accuracy, plating thickness, etching uniformity)
  - Destructive cross-section analysis
  - Supplier qualification verification
- Defects in test coupons indicate potential process drift but do NOT affect the product board.

3. ZONE COORDINATE MAPPING
- Functional area: All coordinates within the product board outline.
- Test coupon area: Coordinates in designated coupon regions (board edges, breakaway tabs).
- When board_zone_map_available=true in the inspection data, use coordinate_range_confirmed_as_test_coupon to determine zone.

4. DISPOSITION BY ZONE
- Defect in functional circuit area: Follow standard defect SOP (reject, rework, or manual review based on defect type).
- Defect in test coupon area:
  - Log for process monitoring — do NOT automatically reject the product.
  - Detection severity from CV applies to the test coupon, not the product.
  - Business disposition depends on board-zone mapping, so CV detection alone should not automatically reject the board.
  - Notify process engineering if defect rate in test coupons exceeds monitoring thresholds.
