TECHNICAL MANUAL
TECH-PCB-X100-REV-B: PCB-X100 Rev-B Technical Specifications and Defect Risk Assessment
Product: PCB-X100 | Revision: Rev-B | Part Number: PN-PCB-X100-REV-B

1. PRODUCT OVERVIEW
PCB-X100 is the Main Control PCB used in vehicle body control and lighting control electronics. It is manufactured at Berlin Electronics Plant (FACTORY-BERLIN-01) on PCB Assembly Line A3 (LINE-A3).

2. MATERIAL SPECIFICATION
- Substrate: FR-4 copper-clad laminate
- Copper weight: 1 oz (35 um) outer layers
- Layer count: 4 layers
- Surface finish: Standard copper trace finish
- Solder mask: Green LPI

3. DEFECT RISK AREAS

3.1 Foreign Material on Base Material:
- Foreign material on base material may affect reliability if located near conductor paths, vias, or coating-sensitive areas.
- Base material areas between conductor traces are susceptible to particle accumulation during handling and inspection.
- Foreign particles in non-conductor areas are generally cosmetic but must be evaluated for proximity to active circuits.

3.2 Conductor Trace Sensitivity:
- Fine conductor traces in the BCM-01 power distribution region are sensitive to mechanical damage, etching variation, and contamination.
- Minimum trace width in critical areas: 0.15mm.
- Trace spacing in dense routing areas: 0.12mm minimum.

3.3 Reflective Copper Regions:
- After etching, exposed copper trace regions can be highly reflective.
- Reflective surfaces may cause false positives in AOI systems, especially after lighting calibration changes.
- Visual sensitivity is high in copper trace finish areas.

4. APPLICATION
- Affected system: Body control module (BCM)
- Subsystem: Lighting control, power distribution
- Safety criticality: Medium (Rev-B standard production), High (power distribution paths)
