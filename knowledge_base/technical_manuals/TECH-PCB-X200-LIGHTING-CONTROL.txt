TECHNICAL MANUAL
TECH-PCB-X200-LIGHTING-CONTROL: PCB-X200 Lighting Control Module Technical Specifications
Product: PCB-X200 | Revision: Rev-A | Part Number: PN-PCB-X200-REV-A

1. PRODUCT OVERVIEW
PCB-X200 is the Lighting Control PCB used in the vehicle lighting control module. It is inspected on secondary PCB inspection line LINE-C2 at station AOI-09.

2. SPECIFICATIONS
- Substrate: FR-4 standard
- Layer count: 2 layers
- Conductor edge sensitivity: Medium
- Application area: Automotive body electronics — lighting control
- Safety criticality: Medium
- Surface finish: HASL (Hot Air Solder Leveling)

3. DEFECT RISK AREAS

3.1 Mouse Bite (MB):
- Mouse Bite near conductor edges may affect reliability, but confirmation is required before disposition.
- MB defects are typically caused by depaneling process issues (routing bit wear, incorrect offset).
- The conductor edges on PCB-X200 have medium sensitivity to MB — minor edge irregularities may be acceptable if conductor width is maintained.

3.2 MB Disposition Guidance:
- If MB is detected with high confidence and is confirmed to reduce conductor width below specification: reject.
- If MB is detected with low confidence: manual review required before disposition.
- If no historical recurrence data exists for MB on this product/line/station, do not assume a recurring pattern.

4. INSPECTION LINE
- Inspected on LINE-C2 (secondary PCB inspection line)
- AOI station: AOI-09
- Different inspection environment from LINE-A3 — separate calibration and maintenance schedules.
