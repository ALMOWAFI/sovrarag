ENGINEERING CHANGE NOTICE
ECN-2026-00510: PCB-X100 Material Revision Change (Rev-B to Rev-C)
Effective Date: 2026-03-01 | Owner: Product Engineering

1. CHANGE SUMMARY
PCB-X100 changed from Rev-B to Rev-C. The primary change is a new substrate supplier (SUP-077) replacing the previous supplier for the base FR-4 laminate material.

2. CHANGE DETAILS
- Part number change: PN-PCB-X100-REV-B to PN-PCB-X100-REV-C
- Substrate material: New FR-4 formulation from SUP-077
- Copper specification: No change
- Layer stackup: No change
- Solder mask: No change
- Electrical specifications: No change (verified by qualification testing)

3. VISUAL IMPACT
- New substrate supplier SUP-077 may show slightly different base material texture during qualification lots.
- Expected visual differences:
  - Slightly different surface texture (smoother or rougher than Rev-B baseline)
  - Minor color shade variation in bare substrate areas
  - These differences are cosmetic and do not affect electrical or mechanical performance.

4. QUALIFICATION REQUIREMENTS
- Minimum 3 production batches must pass qualification criteria (see QA-ACCEPT-REV-C).
- All qualification batches tracked separately in QMS with qualification_batch=true flag.
- Visual variation reports should be logged but do not count as defects unless confirmed by CV or manual inspection.
- Confirmed defect rate on Rev-C must not exceed Rev-B historical baseline by more than 50%.

5. INSPECTION GUIDANCE
- AOI programs do not need modification for Rev-C unless false positive rate increases significantly.
- Operators should be briefed on expected visual differences to reduce unnecessary defect reports.
- If CV inspection returns no defect and manual review confirms no functional issue, the board should be passed.

6. REFERENCES
- QA-ACCEPT-REV-C: Acceptance Criteria for Rev-C
- Supplier qualification report for SUP-077
