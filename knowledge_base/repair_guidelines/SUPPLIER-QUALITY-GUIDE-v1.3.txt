SUPPLIER QUALITY GUIDELINE
SUPPLIER-QUALITY-GUIDE-v1.3: Supplier Quality Investigation and Containment Procedure
Revision: 1.3 | Effective Date: 2025-06-01 | Owner: Supplier Quality Engineering

1. SCOPE
This guideline covers the investigation and containment procedures when defect patterns are linked to a specific supplier material lot.

2. TRIGGER CONDITIONS
- Cross-line or cross-plant recurrence associated with the same material lot requires supplier containment action.
- Specific triggers:
  - Same defect type detected on 2+ production lines with the same supplier lot.
  - Same defect type detected at 2+ factory locations with the same supplier lot.
  - Defect rate on a specific supplier lot exceeds historical baseline by >100%.
  - Internal repair attempts on lot-linked defects show >50% failure rate.

3. CONTAINMENT ACTIONS

3.1 Immediate Actions (within 24 hours of trigger):
Step 1: Quarantine all boards in the affected batch that have not yet passed final inspection.
Step 2: Identify all batches across all lines and factories that used the same supplier lot.
Step 3: Place quarantine hold on all identified batches.
Step 4: Notify supplier quality team and open a Supplier Quality Case (SQC).
Step 5: Stop incoming material from the affected lot.

3.2 Investigation (within 5 business days):
Step 1: Collect defect data across all affected lines and factories.
Step 2: Perform incoming material re-inspection on retained samples from the affected lot.
Step 3: Compare defect characteristics with previous lots from the same supplier.
Step 4: Request supplier 8D investigation report.

3.3 Resolution:
- Do not release quarantined material until supplier investigation is complete.
- Supplier must provide root cause analysis and corrective action plan.
- Corrective action must be verified on next lot delivery before returning to normal quality status.

4. SUPPLIER QUALITY STATUS LEVELS
- normal: No active quality concerns.
- under_review: Active investigation, incoming inspection tightened.
- containment: Active quality case, affected lots quarantined.
- suspended: Supplier shipments halted pending resolution.
